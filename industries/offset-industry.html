Optimal Roller Coverings for Superior Print Quality.

In modern Offset and Web printing, roller coverings are crucial for ensuring high-quality output and making the system more economical to operate.

Roller Technic with its partners has developed and rigorously tested ideal coverings to meet the needs of the most demanding printers, even under the toughest conditions.Our advanced compound formulations provide exceptional resistance to heat, solvents, and abrasion.

These coverings offer dimensional stability, surface elasticity, and softness, ensuring optimal and long-lasting performance.

We offer rubber compounds compatible with traditional inks, UV inks, and the latest series for LED-UV inks.

Conventional inks

The compounds of the SAK S series, for inking and distributor rollers, offer excellent chemical resistance and durability characteristics in the most demanding operating conditions, guaranteeing high and constant print quality. The formulations, developed by the technical staff of the Roller Technic Rubber Compound Division in collaboration with the major printers and machine manufacturers, guarantee:

- Excellent resistance to abrasion and oxidation;
- Low heat build-up;
- Long time dimensional stability;
- Easy to clean;
- Excellent elasticity and surface softness;
- High uniformity in the distribution of the ink

SAK S compounds can be used with HYBRID inks, after technical verification by the laboratory.

UV inks

The UV printing sector has seen significant growth in recent decades, becoming increasingly important in paper converting and metal packaging. Through close collaboration with leading printers, Roller Technic has consistently stayed at the forefront, developing the TECNOLAST V series of special compounds. These compounds offer high resistance to UV inks and the often aggressive washing solvents used in the process.

TECNOLAST V compounds main features:
‍
- High dynamic characteristics,
- Excellent grinding capacity,
- Excellent inking properties,
- High dimensional stability during the time,
- Very low heat build-up,
- Great mechanical and aging resistance.

All the compounds of the TECNOLAST V series are very sensitive to conventional inks and fatty solvents so much that they cannot be used with them even for very short periods.

- TECNOLAST V1130ShA: Light brown – special low hardness, inking and dampening rollers
- TECNOLAST V1335ShA: Light brown – inking and dampening rollers
- TECNOLAST V1445ShA: Light brown – inking and distributor rollers

UV and conventional inks mixed use

The growing market demands for UV printed items have pushed more and more companies to combine this type of printing with the conventional one. Hence the need to have rollers capable of being used with both conventional and UV inks.

The compounds of the SAK M series meet the needs of printers who use U.V. inks or paints. alternately with conventional inks.

The new SAK M series guarantees:

- High chemical resistance to UV inks for paper (no limit of use)
- Good chemical resistance to UV inks for plastic (recommended use 35%)
- Low oxidation
- Easy colour change (conventional / UV)
- Excellent dimensional stability

The SAK M coatings allow the continuous use of hybrid inks.

- SAK M3135ShA: Black color – Inking and dampening rollers
- SAK M4145ShA: Black color – Inking and distributor rollers

Coverings for dampening rollers

The most recent Offset machines are equipped with dampening systems that require the use of low or zero IPA; rubber rollers, metering or transfer, are therefore even more fundamental, they have evolved to guarantee the necessary water flow and the best spreading of the dampening solution.

The new coverings of series IPAR HYDROFILM, successfully tested by prestigious printers, transport the dampening solution with great regularity and constancy, creating a uniform film both in the presence of low IPA and with ZERO IPA.

The latest addition to the series deserves special mention: the IPARTEC coating.

Born from a study on composite materials, the IPARTEC roller is used as metering and guarantees an excellent water flow rate combined with an exceptional chemical inertia, a remarkable ease of cleaning and a stability and durability higher than the average of the other rollers.

- IPARI1028ShA: Dark blue – Metering rollers for conventional inks with low Alcohol – IPA 5 to 8%
- IPAR I1128ShA: Dark blue Metering rollers for conventional inks with low or ZERO Alcohol – IPA <5%
- IPAR I3030ShA: Dark blue Dampening rollers for conventional inks. Metering rollers for conventional and UV.
- IPAR I4040ShA: Dark blue High mechanical properties – Metering rollers for conventional and UV inks.
- IPAR IV3030ShA: Pink Special metering rollers specific for UV inks.
- IPARTEC: Black Metering rollers for conventional and UV inks. Ideal for mixed use. Specific for ZERO alcohol – IPA < 3%.
